How to use

  1. Turn on your Circuit Cubes Bluetooth Hub.
  2. Click Connect Hub and select it from the pairing menu.
  3. Drag the sliders to control speed (-255 to 255).
  4. Snap: When ON, the motor stops instantly when you let go of the slider (perfect for steering).
  5. Invert: Reverses motor direction in software if your build is wired backwards.
